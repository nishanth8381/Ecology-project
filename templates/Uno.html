The U-no Technique

If you live in a hostel you would have probably noticed that the lights in the washroom are mostly switched on throughout the night. Does switching off the lights during night or when no one is inside a washroom make a difference? Turns out it makes a ton of difference!

Power saving Technique using Arduino and Ultrasonic sensors (U-no Counter)

Case Study - 1 (IITTP)

In each washroom of an hostel we have around 2 LED tubelights, 10 ceiling LED's and  5 exhaust fans

Statistics for 1 day before using U-no counter

Items  Power Rating (per item in Watts) Time of usage (hrs) Total power consumed (in KWh) Cost (in rupees )
2 Tubelights 20 24 0.48 4.78
10 ceiling LED's 18 24 0.432 4.3
5 exhaust fans 90 24 2.16 21.5


 

 

Statistics for 1 day after using U-no counter ( Assuming that using a U-no counter reduces 8 hrs consumption per day)

Items  Power Rating (per item in Watts) Time of usage (hrs) Total power consumed (in KWh) Cost (in rupees )

Case Study - 2

Let's say we implement this technique in a huge institiute ( like IIT Bombay ) having around 400 washrooms (All these washrooms are assumed to have same power consumption)

Per Academic year (2 sems/ 8 months):

Attribute Without U-no counter Using U-no counter
Total power usage time (hrs) 5760 3840
Total power consumed (KWh) 294912 196800
Total Cost (rupees) 2935680 1958400
Total power saved: 98112 units
Total money saved: 9,77,280 rupees
Reduction in CO2 emmision: 88,300.8 Kg

 

 





If we consider the net profit, the cost of installation of these U-no filters in 400 washrooms is about 400*450 = 1,80,000 rupees. Net profit would still be 7,97,280 rupees!

The money saved due to them is much larger than this value as this is one-time installation and has very less maintanence costs.

Let's look at the bigger picture. If this 98112 units of power is saved, it means that about 88,300 kg of CO2 emmision is reduced in 8 months in the institute, Just imagine the reduction in Carbon-footprint if such methods are implemented in all large institutions in the world!
